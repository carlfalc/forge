# Forge

Forge is a geopolitical event trading system that converts breaking news into actionable forex and commodity trades within seconds. As tensions escalate globally — Ukraine war, China-Taiwan standoff, Middle East conflicts — traditional traders miss critical entry points while news breaks. Forge uses real-time news feeds, sentiment analysis, and pre-configured trade templates to execute positions automatically when specific geopolitical triggers occur.

## About

Professional traders lose millions in opportunity cost when geopolitical events move markets faster than human reaction time. When Russia invaded Ukraine, EUR/USD dropped 200 pips in 3 minutes — retail traders were still reading headlines while institutions had already positioned. Commodity traders face the same issue: oil spikes 5% on pipeline attacks, gold jumps on central bank announcements, wheat futures explode on export bans. Current solutions require manual monitoring of 50+ news sources, then manual analysis, then manual execution. By the time a human processes 'Israeli airstrikes on Iranian facilities,' the USD/JPY safe-haven trade is already over. Traders need split-second positioning on breaking geopolitical events, but lack institutional-grade news processing infrastructure.

## Tech Stack

I scored 7 because: news feed APIs are well-established (Reuters, Bloomberg), broker APIs are standardized (MT5, cTrader, Interactive Brokers), and natural language processing for event detection is mature technology. Main technical challenges are latency optimization, false positive filtering, and risk management systems, but these are solved problems in fintech. 3-month MVP timeline realistic for core functionality with 6-month timeline for production-ready risk controls.

## Point of Difference

First system to offer millisecond geopolitical trade execution for retail traders. Pre-built templates for 47 geopolitical scenarios (invasion, sanctions, central bank emergency, commodity export bans). Natural language event detection — understands 'Iran closes Strait of Hormuz' should trigger long oil, short EUR. Risk-aware position sizing based on event severity and account balance. Integration with every major retail broker API. Unlike generic news traders, we specialize purely in geopolitical catalysts with proven market impact.
